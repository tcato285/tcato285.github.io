I am in a program called the Upstate Young Marines. At the current moment, I am a Sergeant and have a platoon under my command. I also lead in PT and participate as a rifleman in the colorguard.

The Young Marines is a national youth organization for kids ranging from 8 to the completion of high school. It focuses on the core principles of leadership, teamwork, and discipline. Among the many aspects the Young Marins focus on, they teach children about the harmful effects of drugs.
